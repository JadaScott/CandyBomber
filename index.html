Our Mission

The Gail S. Halvorsen Aviation Education Foundation advances aviation education, promotes youth leadership development, enhances community capacity for emergency response, and encourages humanitarian service on macro- and micro-levels.

Gail's Story

The Gail S. Halvorsen Aviation Education Foundation was formed in 2016 by a group of community-minded volunteers who seek to perpetuate the great legacy of The Candy Bomber story.

The Foundation

We are grateful to all of those who donate to the foundation. Visit our support page for more information on our sponsors and how to donate to the foundation.

Educators

We strive to help educators find enriching STEM Activities for their students. From videos, hands on activities, and lesson plans, we hope to inspire more love for STEM in the classroom.
